# Code of Conduct
## Overview
All contributors to FNF Prompt Library must comply with company policies, security standards, Responsible AI principles, and applicable laws when creating, sharing, or maintaining prompts and related assets.
## Core Requirements
### Data Protection & Confidentiality
- No PII, PHI, financial, customer, or employee data
- No confidential, proprietary (project related), or restricted internal information
- Sensitive system instructions must be abstracted or protected
### Responsible AI & Ethics
- Avoid biased, discriminatory, offensive, or harmful language
- Do not encourage unethical, illegal, or unsafe behavior
- Include guardrails to steer outputs toward responsible use


### Quality & Reliability
- Prompts must be clear, deterministic, and well-structured
- Edge cases and failure modes must be considered
- Output format and constraints must be explicitly defined
### Testing & Validation
- Test prompts with representative inputs and edge cases
- Review outputs for accuracy, safety, and consistency
- Document known limitations and risks
### Documentation & Metadata
- Document model compatibility and dependencies
## Professional Conduct
Contributors are expected to:
- Collaborate respectfully and professionally
- Accept constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the organization
